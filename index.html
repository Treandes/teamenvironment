Education

Many problems arise due to a lack of education. If more people in low-income neighborhoods were able to get quality education, many more would be able to escape the cycle of poverty.

Housing

With the history of housing projects, people of color are basically trapped in bad neighborhoods and are unable to get out because of the institutionalized racism. Many people of color are not able to get housing in predominately white neighborhoods, even if they can afford it, because of gentrification and the predisposed opinions of People of colored.
